Eligibility

To write the Recreational Pilot Permit (Aeroplane) RPPAE exam, you must meet the following requirements:

Medical category

This exam requires a medical category 1 or 3 certificate valid on or after your exam date of July 4th, 2023.

Your certificate must have been issued within the last 5 years if under the age of 40 or 2 years if 40 years of age or older.

Previous experience

A minimum of {10 hours of flight time} is needed.

Flight training unit letter or foreign licence

A letter of recommendation from an accredited flight training unit or proof of foreign licensing is required.

Other prerequisites

  • {You are an active or retired member of the Canadian Forces and have qualified to pilot wings standard.}

Previous exam attempts

If you've attempted this exam before, you may only rewrite it:

  • 14 days after the first attempt
  • 30 days after the second attempt
  • 30 days, plus an additional 30 days for each attempt afterwards up to 180 days
