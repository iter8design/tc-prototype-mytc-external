{First name} {Last name} Register a drone

You must register to operate any drone with an operating weight over 250 g within Canada. Benefits of registration include:

  • If you lose your drone, registration may help in returning it to you if it is found
  • If there is a recall from the manufacturer, we may be able to notify you

There is a $10 non-refundable fee to register a drone.

Eligibility

Any owner of a drone that weighs 250 g and over.

Drones under 250 g do not need to be registered. Drones over 150 kg require a special flight operations certificate instead.

What you need before you start

It should take 3 minutes to register a drone. To begin, you will need the following information:

  • purchase date (if applicable)
  • make, model, serial number, weight and type of drone
  • a Visa®, Mastercard®, or American Express® credit card, Apple Pay® or Google Pay®
    • Debit cards may be used if added to your Apple Wallet® app, or Google Pay® app.
